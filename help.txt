"My Carrom" is a simple game based on carrom.

The rules are pretty simple:

1. Player chooses the number of coins of each color
2. Color he/she wants to play for
3. Difficulty level i.e. roughness of the surface
4. Timer
5. Score decrements by one every 5 seconds
6. It is necessary to pocket the queen before pocketing all coins of his/her color
7. Other color's coin is placed at the center if pocketed

Points:

+10 for pocketing coin of his color
-5 for pocketing coin of other color
+50 for pocketing coin of queen
-10 for pocketing coin of striker

User inputs for striker(moves):

a to change direction anticlockwise
d to change the direction clockwise
left key to move striker leftwards
right key to move striker rightwards
spacebar to shoot

Mouse inputs:

left button press: set the direction for the striker
left button release: shoot
right button press: move the striker horizontally 
cursor up and down: increase and decrease the speed
